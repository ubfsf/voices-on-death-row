## 🎯 Key Design Decisions

### Visual Aesthetic
**Decision**: Documentary-style, minimal design
**Why**: Respects the serious subject matter, focuses on content over flashiness
**Result**: Professional, intimate, archive-like feel

### Color Palette
**Decision**: Cream paper (#fcfaf7) + dark ink (#1c1917)
**Why**: Mimics aged documents, feels authentic and archival
**Result**: Calming, readable, respectful

### Typography
**Decision**: Serif for headlines (Cormorant Garamond), sans-serif for body (Inter)
**Why**: Artistic serif conveys importance, readable sans-serif for accessibility
**Result**: Professional yet personal feel

### Animation Approach
**Decision**: Scroll-triggered reveals using Framer Motion
**Why**: Guided reading experience, paced storytelling
**Result**: Immersive without being distracting

### Content Management
**Decision**: Sanity CMS over custom admin panel
**Why**: Professional, scalable, non-technical users can add content
**Result**: Halima can manage content without coding
